# Mission

## Who We Are

We run a small native plant nursery in Lancaster County, Pennsylvania. Our mission goes beyond selling plants — we are working to cover Lancaster County in native plantings.

## The 25% Strategy

One of our core business and environmental strategies is targeting sympathetic homeowners street by street, with the goal of reaching **25% native garden coverage per neighborhood**. Research suggests this is a social tipping point: once 1 in 4 homes has a native garden, it becomes socially normal and acceptable to the rest of the neighborhood, accelerating adoption without us needing to intervene house by house.

To execute this strategy, we need to know:
- Which houses already have native plants
- Which streets are close to the 25% threshold (high-value targets)
- Which areas we haven't surveyed yet

## How We Gather Data

We walk neighborhoods. When we pass a house, we tap Yes or No on our phones. That's it. The app records our GPS location, the street address, whether the house has native plants, the date, and which of us logged it.

## Who Uses This App

Two people: the two owners of the nursery (sisters). Both use iPhones. We always have cell signal when walking in Lancaster County. There are no other users, no volunteers, no public access.

## Privacy

Survey data is proprietary business intelligence. GPS coordinates and yes/no records for individual houses must never be publicly accessible. Only the two authenticated owners may ever read or write data. This is a hard requirement.

## Guiding Principles

- **Free to operate.** We are a small business. Every component must have a free tier that covers our usage indefinitely. Do not introduce paid services.
- **Simple to use.** Neither owner has a coding background. The app must work without any technical knowledge to operate day-to-day.
- **Mobile-first.** The primary use case is an iPhone while walking outdoors. Large tap targets, minimal typing, works in Safari.
- **Lightweight.** The app should be fast and not drain phone battery unnecessarily.
- **Phased.** We expect to build this incrementally. Get the core field tool working first, then the dashboard, then analytics.
